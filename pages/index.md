I am Athena Vizuete. I am a History major and Women, Gender, and Sexuality Studies; and Public History and Museum Studies minor. I am interested in Labor history and the mid- to late-19th- and early-20th-century history in the United States. I am a Lumen Scholar and College Fellow at Elon University, and I am creating a museum exhibit with the African American Cultural Arts and History Center in Burlington, North Carolina.
